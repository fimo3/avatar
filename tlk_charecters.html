Characters in "The Legend of Korra"

Air Nomad characters

Tenzin

Role: Tenzin is the youngest son of Avatar Aang and Katara and serves as Korra's airbending teacher and mentor. He is a prominent figure in the series, guiding Korra in her journey to master airbending and connect with her spiritual side. Abilities: Tenzin is a master airbender, one of the few in the world following the events of "Avatar: The Last Airbender." He is highly skilled in traditional airbending techniques, emphasizing agility, evasion, and the use of wind to defend and attack. Tenzin also demonstrates strong leadership and wisdom, playing a crucial role in the revival of the Air Nation and the spiritual guidance of Korra and other characters.

Bumi

Role: Bumi is the eldest son of Aang and Katara. Initially a non-bender and a retired commander of the United Forces, Bumi gains airbending abilities following Harmonic Convergence and becomes an important ally in the series. Abilities: After gaining airbending, Bumi trains and becomes proficient in the skill, though he often uses unconventional and improvised techniques. His military background and resourcefulness make him a valuable member of Team Avatar. Bumi's airbending abilities, combined with his strategic mind and leadership experience, contribute significantly to the group's efforts in maintaining balance and fighting threats.

Zaheer

Role: Zaheer is the main antagonist in the third season and the leader of the Red Lotus, a group of anarchists aiming to dismantle the established order and bring about a new world of freedom through chaos. Abilities: Zaheer becomes an airbender after Harmonic Convergence, despite having no previous bending abilities. He quickly masters airbending and adapts it to his martial arts skills, making him a highly dangerous opponent. Zaheer is also a skilled combatant and strategist. His most notable ability is the power of flight, which he gains by achieving a state of enlightenment and letting go of all earthly attachments, a rare and advanced airbending technique.

Water tribe characters

Korra

Role: Korra is the main protagonist and the current Avatar, succeeding Aang. She is responsible for maintaining balance in the world. Abilities: As the Avatar, Korra can bend all four elements: water, earth, fire, and air. She is a highly skilled and powerful bender, known for her strong physical prowess and combat abilities. Korra also learns to enter the Avatar State, which amplifies her bending abilities, and she gains spiritual abilities such as energy bending and spirit projection over the course of the series.

Kya

Role: Kya is the daughter of Avatar Aang and Katara, and she plays a supportive role in the series. She is a member of the new Team Avatar and helps Korra in her quests. Abilities: Kya is a master waterbender and a skilled healer, having been trained by her mother, Katara. She uses her waterbending abilities in combat effectively and also provides healing to injured allies. Her expertise in waterbending makes her a valuable asset in battles and during times of recovery.

Amon

Role: Princess of the Northern Water Tribe Role: Amon is the main antagonist in the first season of "The Legend of Korra." He is the leader of the Equalists, a revolutionary group seeking to end the oppression of non-benders by benders. Abilities: Amon has the rare and terrifying ability to take away a person's bending permanently, which he claims is a gift from the spirits. He is also a skilled bloodbender, able to use this ability to control and manipulate others without a full moon, which is normally required for such a technique. This ability allows him to subdue opponents easily and maintain his dominance over the Equalists.

Fire Nation characters

Zuko

Role: Zuko, the former Fire Lord and a key character from "Avatar: The Last Airbender," appears in "The Legend of Korra" as an elder statesman. He provides wisdom and support to the new generation of heroes. Abilities: Zuko is a master firebender, with decades of experience and exceptional skill. He can also generate and redirect lightning, showcasing his advanced firebending abilities. Even in his older age, Zuko remains a formidable warrior and leader, using his firebending prowess and strategic mind to aid Korra and her friends in their struggles.

Mako

Role: Mako is a central character and a member of Team Avatar. He is the older brother of Bolin and serves as a close friend and protector of Korra throughout the series. Abilities: Mako is a skilled firebender, with the ability to create and control fire for both combat and practical uses. He is also one of the few known lightning benders, capable of generating and redirecting lightning, a rare and advanced firebending technique. Mako's resourcefulness and strong sense of duty make him an invaluable ally to Korra and the team.

Ghazan

Role: Ghazan is a member of the Red Lotus, a group of anarchists seeking to overthrow the world's governments and bring chaos to the established order. He is one of the main antagonists in the third season. Abilities: Ghazan is a powerful earthbender with the rare ability to lavabend, which allows him to liquefy and manipulate molten rock. This ability makes him an extremely dangerous opponent, capable of causing widespread destruction. Ghazan's lavabending skills are used to devastating effect in battles against Team Avatar, making him a key threat during the Red Lotus's campaign.

Earth Kingdom characters

Bolin

Role: Bolin is a central character and a member of Team Avatar. He is the younger brother of Mako and serves as one of Korra's closest friends and allies. Abilities: Bolin is an earthbender with exceptional talent. Over the course of the series, he learns lavabending, a rare and powerful sub-skill of earthbending that allows him to manipulate molten rock. This ability makes him a formidable opponent in battle. Bolin is also known for his strength, agility, and sense of humor, which endears him to his friends and fans alike.

Suyin Beifong

Role: Suyin, often referred to as "Su," is the matriarch of the Beifong family and the founder of the metalbending city of Zaofu. She plays a significant role in the series, particularly in the third and fourth seasons. Abilities: Suyin is a master earthbender and metalbender, having been trained by her mother, Toph Beifong. Her metalbending skills are extraordinary, allowing her to manipulate metal with precision and power. Suyin also demonstrates agility and proficiency in combat, using her bending abilities to protect her city and assist Korra and her friends in various battles.

Kuvira

Role: Legendary Avatar Role: Kuvira is the main antagonist of the fourth season. Initially introduced as a loyal lieutenant of Suyin, she eventually becomes a dictator with ambitions to unite the Earth Kingdom under her rule, using force if necessary. Abilities: Kuvira is a master metalbender and a highly skilled combatant. Her abilities in metalbending are exceptional, allowing her to bend metal with speed and precision. She is also adept at using metal cables to subdue opponents and enhance her mobility in battle. Kuvira's strategic mind and leadership skills make her a formidable leader and a significant threat to the balance of the world.
